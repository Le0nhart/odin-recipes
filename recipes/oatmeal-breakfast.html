Oatmeal Breakfast

Sweet and healthy Oatmeal breakfast that covers your morning protein and omegas!.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup dry quick oats unsweetened ( this will make 3 servings)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 whole banana
  • 1/4th cup frozen blueberries
  • 2 table spoons unsweetened vegan yogurt
  • 2 table spoons pumpkin seeds
  • 2 table spoons shredded almonds
  • 1 table spoon chia seeds

Start your morning right with all the essential nutrients needed to fuel your body. The oatmeal provides fiber, protein and carbs, alongside the yogurt for digestive aide, the nuts and seeds for healthy fats and the fruits for additional vitamins and amino acids. This is one unbeatable dish for the mornings.

Steps

  1. Pour 1 cup of water into a pot
  2. Place pot on stove and set temperature to medium
  3. Place the dried oats into the water and mix, let cook for about 5-6 minutes stirring every minute or so
  4. Serve your preferred amount of oatmeal into a bowl
  5. Add pumpkin seeds, chia seeds, and almond shreds to the bowl
  6. Place yogurt table spoons over the oatmeal and add your fruits
  7. Enjoy your breakfast!
